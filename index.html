About The Podcast

"Light & Origin" is a thought-provoking podcast hosted by Josh Stafki, dedicated to unraveling the complexities of our world and the human experience. Each episode ventures into diverse topics, from ancient wisdom to modern breakthroughs, seeking to illuminate the origins of ideas, beliefs, and the very fabric of our reality.

Join us as we engage with fascinating guests, explore compelling narratives, and challenge conventional thinking. Whether you're seeking knowledge, inspiration, or simply a new perspective, "Light & Origin" offers a space for curious minds to connect and grow.
